4.

Thoi hieu khoi kien — THAY DOI LON NHAT

Nen ap dung
Ban goc Luat su sua
Thoi hieu khoi kien tranh chap hop dong la 3 nam ke tu ngay den han tra. Nhung theo BLDS 2015, Dieu 155, yeu cau bao ve quyen so huu khong bi gioi han thoi hieu. Nghia la qua 3 nam ban van doi duoc no goc — chi mat quyen doi tien lai. Theo Dieu 429 Bo luat Dan su 2015 thoi hieu khoi kien tranh chap hop dong vay se duoc hieu la 3 nam ke tu ngay ma ban biet quyen va loi ich hop phap cua minh bi xam hai (ngay den han tra ma ho khong tra tien). Trong truong hop het thoi hieu khoi kien doi no thi ben cho vay no van co the khoi kien doi tra lai khoan tien no goc tuy nhien quyen loi cua ban da bi han che di rat nhieu nen hay dac biet ghi nho moc thoi gian nay.
Danh gia — Chinh xac hon:
- Dieu 429 la can cu dung cho thoi hieu hop dong vay. Ban goc dung Dieu 155 (bao ve quyen so huu) — co the gay tranh cai vi quan he vay la quan he nghia vu, khong phai so huu
- Dien dat "quyen loi bi han che di rat nhieu" than trong hon "chi mat quyen doi tien lai"
- Nen ap dung thay doi nay
5.

Thu tuc khoi kien — Mat thong tin cu the

Can trao doi
Ban goc (chi tiet) Luat su sua (gon)
Ban nop don khoi kien tai Toa an nhan dan cap huyen noi nguoi vay cu tru. Toa thu ly trong 5 ngay lam viec. Thoi gian giai quyet thuong tu 4 den 6 thang. An phi dan su tinh theo gia tri tranh chap — vi du khoan vay 100 trieu, tam ung an phi khoang 2,5 trieu dong. Neu thang kien, ben thua chiu toan bo. Ban nop hoan toan co the tu minh hoac nho Luat su nop don khoi kien yeu cau tai Toa an nhan dan co tham quyen de giai quyet.
Van de: Ban goc co thong tin rat thuc te (toa cap huyen, 5 ngay thu ly, 4-6 thang, an phi 2.5 trieu/100 trieu). Ban sua bo het, chi ghi chung chung "TAND co tham quyen".

Con so cu the giup nguoi nghe hinh dung duoc quy trinh, la diem manh cua ban goc.

Cau hoi cho Luat su:

  • Co nen giu lai thong tin "toa cap huyen noi nguoi vay cu tru, thu ly 5 ngay, 4-6 thang, an phi ~2.5 trieu/100 trieu" khong? Day la thong tin rat huu ich cho nguoi nghe.
  • Hay Luat su thay thong tin nay khong chinh xac / co the gay hieu nham nen bo?

7.

Ket bai

Can trao doi
Ban goc Luat su sua
Neu ban dang trong tinh huong tuong tu — dung voi nghi la mat. Kiem tra lai tin nhan, sao ke ngan hang, moi dau vet lien quan. Va nho: lan sau cho ai vay, du than den may — hay chuyen khoan, ghi ro noi dung, giu lai tin nhan xac nhan. Do khong phai thieu tin tuong — do la bao ve ca hai ben. Khi cho vay tien, du than den may — hay chuyen khoan, ghi ro noi dung, giu lai tin nhan xac nhan. Do khong phai thieu tin tuong — do la bao ve ca hai ben truoc nhung tranh cai khi "tri nho" moi ben khong giong nhau hoac don gian la bao ve ban than khi rui ro xay ra, chung ta van hoan toan co the chung minh duoc.

Hy vong cau chuyen hom nay giup co them kien thuc de phong ngua rui ro trong viec cho vay va tranh duoc nhung sai lam khong dang co. Neu can duoc tu van sau hon ve giao dich vay - cho vay, hay lien he voi chung toi de duoc Luat su tu van day du chi tiet hon.

Luat su vi ban — dong hanh cung ban!
Diem hay: "bao ve ca hai ben truoc nhung tranh cai khi tri nho moi ben khong giong nhau" — giai thich "tai sao" phai lam, rat thuyet phuc.
Can trao doi:
- Ban goc co doan "dung voi nghi la mat. Kiem tra lai tin nhan, sao ke..." — loi khuyen thuc te, rat huu ich. Ban sua bo mat doan nay.
- CTA "Neu can tu van sau hon... hay lien he voi chung toi" → xu ly trong outro audio, khong can de trong content
- "Luat su vi ban — dong hanh cung ban!" → day la slogan moi? Co dung cho Vibalaw khong?

Cau hoi cho Luat su:

  • "Luat su vi ban — dong hanh cung ban!" la slogan chinh thuc muon dung cho Vibalaw khong? Hay chi la ket nganh chung?
  • Co nen giu lai loi khuyen "dung voi nghi la mat, kiem tra lai tin nhan, sao ke ngan hang" tu ban goc?

Tong ket — Hanh dong de xuat

Ap dung ngay (8 diem chinh xac hon):

  • 1 Bo sung "Khoan 1" cho Dieu 119 BLDS 2015
  • 2 Bo sung Dieu 7 Luat GDDT 2023 — giao dich dien tu = giao dich bang van ban
  • 3 Sua "co gia tri ngang giay vay no" → "co the co gia tri nhu mot loi xac nhan vay no" (than trong hon)
  • 4 Bo sung Khoan 1 Dieu 94, Khoan 2 Dieu 95, Khoan 5 Dieu 95 BLTTDS 2015
  • 5 Thoi hieu: dung Dieu 429 BLDS 2015 thay vi Dieu 155 (chinh xac hon)
  • 6 Them luu y khi lap vi bang — danh gia noi dung, tranh bo sot
  • 7 Sai lam 3: them "tac dong vat ly" + "ben dung bong thanh sai"
  • 8 Ket bai: "bao ve ca hai ben truoc nhung tranh cai khi tri nho moi ben khong giong nhau"

  • 1 Do dai: Dong y cat giam ve ≤580 tu? Phan nao bat buoc giu?

  • 3 Slogan: "Luat su vi ban — dong hanh cung ban" la slogan chinh thuc?
  • 4 Ket bai: Giu loi khuyen "dung voi nghi la mat, kiem tra lai tin nhan" tu ban goc?
